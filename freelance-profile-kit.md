## Questions To Ask Clients

- What type of business is this for?
- What services should appear on the page?
- What information should clients submit?
- Do you need a dashboard or only form submissions?
- Should leads include traffic sources?
- Do you already have branding, colors or logo?
- Do you need deployment help?
- Do you need database, email or Telegram notifications?

## Do Not Say

- I am a beginner
- I can do anything
- I will work for very cheap
- Please give me a chance

- I can build a focused first version
- I can adapt my existing booking dashboard template
- I can help you collect and manage client requests
